About

is a statically typed compiled language touted as 'C for the 21st century' due in part to its development taking place after the advent of the internet and distributed computing.

Initial Development

Took place between Rob pike (C/UTF 8) Robert Griesemer (Hotspot JVM) Ken Thompson (Unix/Bsd) at Google starting in 2007. Many languages and frameworks were needed to maintain the large distributed computing infrastructure google had. There was a desire for a modern language that could handle just about anything.

  • Distribituted Systems
