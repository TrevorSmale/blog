Tympan

Open Audio Device

Updated 2022/01/28



Description

The Tympan project is an Open group project aspiring to design and standardize a free and open hearing aid / Audiology tester platform.

The Story

Until very recently, assistive technologies for hearing loss were out of reach due to high costs and complex fitting/tuning procedures. Now, due to the convergence of low cost open source micro electronics and skillful altruistic developers, there exists the Tympan project. A team of highly skilled individuals have created a universal hearing aid platform using relatively simple and accessible parts. project Tympan has developed a small device that hangs around the neck rather than sit in the ear. Audio is captured through an inbuilt microphone, then amplified and modulated to suite the hearing profile of the user. The audio output signal is either played through run of the mill headphones or transmitted via bluetooth.
Hearing loss is a broadly experienced phenomenon, afflicting people of all ages. It is my hope that this project reaches more eyes. If you are looking to endorse a simple project that can have an enormously positive effect, please visit the official tympan project website.
